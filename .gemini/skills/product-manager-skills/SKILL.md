# Product Manager Skills

This skill provides access to a professional library of product management frameworks and workflows. It is organized into several key domains.

## How to Use
When you need to apply a specific framework, read the corresponding `SKILL.md` file in the `references/` directory for detailed instructions and patterns.

### 1. Strategy & Business Health
- **Product Strategy Session**: `references/product-strategy-session/SKILL.md` - Full strategy cycle.
- **Business Health Diagnostic**: `references/business-health-diagnostic/SKILL.md` - SaaS health check.
- **Market Sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM)**: `references/tam-sam-som-calculator/SKILL.md`.
- **Positioning**: `references/positioning-statement/SKILL.md` and `references/positioning-workshop/SKILL.md`.

### 2. Prioritization & Roadmapping
- **Prioritization Advisor**: `references/prioritization-advisor/SKILL.md` - Choose between RICE, ICE, etc.
- **Roadmap Planning**: `references/roadmap-planning/SKILL.md`.
- **Feature Investment**: `references/feature-investment-advisor/SKILL.md`.

### 3. Discovery & Validation
- **Discovery Process**: `references/discovery-process/SKILL.md`.
- **Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD)**: `references/jobs-to-be-done/SKILL.md`.
- **Opportunity Solution Tree**: `references/opportunity-solution-tree/SKILL.md`.
- **Proof of Life (PoL) Probes**: `references/pol-probe/SKILL.md`.

### 4. Product Artifacts & Delivery
- **PRD Development**: `references/prd-development/SKILL.md`.
- **User Stories**: `references/user-story/SKILL.md` and `references/user-story-splitting/SKILL.md`.
- **User Story Mapping**: `references/user-story-mapping/SKILL.md`.
- **Amazon-style Press Release**: `references/press-release/SKILL.md`.

### 5. Metrics & Finance
- **SaaS Growth Metrics**: `references/saas-revenue-growth-metrics/SKILL.md`.
- **SaaS Efficiency Metrics**: `references/saas-economics-efficiency-metrics/SKILL.md`.
- **Finance Quick Reference**: `references/finance-metrics-quickref/SKILL.md`.

## Workflow for Agent
1. **Identify** the user's need (e.g., "help me prioritize tasks" or "write a PRD").
2. **Select** the appropriate framework from the list above.
3. **Read** the specific `SKILL.md` from the `references/` folder.
4. **Follow** the instructions within that specific skill to guide the user.
